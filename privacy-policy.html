Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

AI Longevity App ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your personal and health information when you use our application and related services (collectively, the "Service"). This policy applies to all users of the Service, regardless of location.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information. When you create an account, we collect your first name, last name, and email address.

Health and Wellness Data. With your explicit consent, we collect health metrics from connected wearable devices and platforms, including but not limited to:

  • Sleep data (duration, stages, quality scores)
  • Heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Activity and step counts
  • Blood oxygen saturation (SpO2)
  • Stress and recovery metrics
  • Body temperature and respiratory rate

Device and Usage Data. We automatically collect device type, operating system, app version, IP address, browser type, and general usage analytics to improve the Service.

Payment Information. If you purchase a subscription or premium features through in-app purchases (Apple App Store or Google Play Store), payment is processed entirely by Apple or Google. We do not collect or store your credit card number, bank account details, or other financial information. We receive only a transaction confirmation and subscription status from the platform.

2. How We Collect Your Data

We collect data through the following methods:

  • Directly from you — when you create an account, update your profile, or contact us.
  • From wearable integrations — through authorized OAuth 2.0 connections with Oura, WHOOP, Fitbit (Google), and Garmin. You explicitly grant permission before any data is shared with us, and you can revoke access at any time through the respective platform’s settings or through our app.
  • Automatically — through analytics and tracking technologies when you use the Service (see Section 5).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized health and longevity insights
  • Display trends and analytics based on your wearable data
  • Process transactions and manage your subscriptions
  • Send you account-related communications (e.g., password resets, subscription confirmations)
  • Send you optional product updates, tips, and newsletters (you may opt out at any time)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve and develop new features
  • Ensure the security and integrity of the Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal or health data to third parties. We do not use your health data for advertising or remarketing purposes.
